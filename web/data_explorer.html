ü§î Why These Exclusions?

  • Buildings (3m): Prevents foundation damage, ensures sunlight access, avoids roof/gutter interference
  • Parking (9,215 areas): Ensures accessibility, prevents vehicle damage, maintains traffic flow
  • Roads (2.5m): Maintains sight lines, prevents root damage to pavement, ensures pedestrian safety
  • Existing Trees (dynamic): Prevents competition for water/nutrients, maintains canopy health, varies by crown size
  • Fire Access (5m): Legal requirement for emergency vehicle access, fire safety code compliance
  • Water Bodies: Ecological protection, flood management, impossible terrain
